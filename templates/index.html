Convert photos taken via Snapmatic in PC version of GTA V into ordinary JPEG images. This can be done directly without the use of Rockstar Social Club. In fact those are the files named PGTA<...> (without extension) inside your C:\Users\<UserName>\Documents\Rockstar Games\GTA V\Profiles\<PROFILEID>\ directory.

For cracked version you can check for either into
C:\Users\<UserName>\AppData\Roaming\Goldberg SocialClub Emu Saves\GTA V
or into C:\ProgramData\Socialclub\YOURUSERNAME
